Who Am I & My Experience

My Biography

My name is Timur Barkov, and I am a student interested in engineering and robotics. I became interested in engineering after participating in a robotics competition in Italy, where I saw how ideas can be turned into real working systems through technology and teamwork. Since then, I developed a strong motivation to continue in this field. I am interested in engineering because it is practical, in demand, and directly connected to real-world problems. I especially enjoy building systems, working with robotics, and understanding how things work.

My Goals

In the future, I see myself working in robotics or AI systems, developing both engineering and programming skills. My goal is to create robots and intelligent systems that are useful in real life and bring innovation. For me, the most important things are freedom, innovation, and doing what I enjoy. I want to work on meaningful projects that I truly believe in. I also have very ambitious goals — to build my own projects and startups and turn them into successful companies in the future. I am also highly motivated to join your school, as I believe it will give me the right environment to grow academically, develop my skills, and achieve my goals in engineering.

My Story

I see myself mainly as a hardware engineer and designer. I enjoy designing, building, and improving systems, especially in robotics projects. In team work, I take responsibility for my tasks, complete them independently, and then ask for feedback to improve. After finishing my part, I help others when needed to improve the overall result. One of my strengths is combining creativity and logic. I often contribute ideas during brainstorming, and many of them help improve the final solution. What I enjoy most is the moment when a project is finished before the deadline, and the team can see the result of its work.

My hobbies

My hobbies reflect my interests in creativity, technology, and sports, and they help me grow both personally and professionally.
Sports

Sports

Sports are an important part of my life. Gym training helps me stay disciplined, healthy, and balanced while managing studies and projects. Basketball improves my teamwork, reaction speed, and decision-making, and I also gained experience playing for my school team, especially in teamwork and pressure situations. Earlier, I practiced martial arts, which helped me build discipline and focus.

Design

Design

Design is one of my creative passions. I work with Canva, Blender, and 3D printing to create posters, UI concepts, and 3D models. My designs are often connected to competitions and personal projects. I enjoy turning simple ideas into detailed visual or physical prototypes, combining creativity with practical thinking.

Robotics

Robotics

I have strong experience in robotics, working with Arduino, LEGO robotics, ESP32, Raspberry Pi, and AI-based systems. I combine hardware and software to build smart, interactive machines and regularly develop robots for competitions and personal projects. I have also participated in international robotics competitions, where I developed teamwork skills and the ability to perform under pressure. I plan to pursue a career in building and improving real-world systems.

Programming

Programming

I have solid programming skills in Python, Swift, UIKit, and HTML. I enjoy building apps, websites, and solving problems through code, turning ideas into real working products. Programming is closely connected to my robotics projects, where I use it to build and control smart systems. I also create iOS apps, websites, and small tools, and it plays a key role in most of my projects and ideas.
